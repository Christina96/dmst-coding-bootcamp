About the University

    The Athens University of Economics and Business was formed in 1920 as the School of Commercial Studies and renamed in 1926 in School of Economics and Business (ASOEE). Since 1989 is the current name Athens University of Economics and Business (OPA). The OPA is the third Higher Education institution in the country and first in the field of Economics and Business Administration. It has a rich history of important scientific achievements that characterize the modern present and prescribing excellent prospects for the future. It has three faculties which included eight departments. One of these sections is the Department of Management Science and Technology.

    The Department of Management Science and Technology was founded in 1999 and is unique in specializing in Greece [combines modern management science with new technologies (IT, communications) and organizational studies] University Department. The mission of the department is education and research in Management Science associated with modern technologies and organizational studies, emphasizing the use of quantitative methods, information technology and communication in decision making, in shaping business strategy and the reorganization of business .. It is one of the eight sections of the Athens University of Economics in Business Administration faculty. In section made Undergraduate, Masters and PhD degree programs and significant research activity.
